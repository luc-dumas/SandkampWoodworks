Established in 1991,

Sandkamp Woodworks has become a well respected cabinet and architectural woodworking business in the New York Metro Area. Working with top designers and architects, Sandkamp Woodworks has been featured in Architectural Digest, The New York Times, Interior Design Magazine, Architectural Record, New York Magazine and The New York Post among others. Our work has been seen on HGTV’s “Dream Builders” and The Style Network’s “Guess Who’s Coming to Decorate”.
